MANA SURGE READY · GUARD CRACK WARNING

Mechanic of the day: Reflect Window Duel

Controls: Mage: Cast / Blink / Ward. Fighter: Slash / Dash / Parry.

Rules: Mage firebolt travels slowly and owns space. Fighter parry has a 10-frame perfect window that reflects it as a fast gold bolt; late parry becomes chip block and pushes fighter back.

Win condition: Best of 5 stocks, 60-second rounds, instant rematch.

Test: Can both players understand “perfect reflect vs safe block” from color, hitstop, sound, and knockback without tutorial text?

Juice checklist

  • Palette-flash characters cyan/gold/red on cast, perfect parry, guard crack.
  • 8-frame hitstop on reflect; 3-frame rumble/screen nudge on blocked spell.
  • Projectile trail uses chunky 2x2 pixels, never same hue as health UI.
  • Round start callout: “READ THE HAND, NOT THE HAT!”
  • Training toggle: show parry window ghost boxes and rollback ping badge.
  • Clip-bait end screen: spell reflected distance, parry timing, rematch button pulsing.
